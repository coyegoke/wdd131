Our Story

Independent Bookstore was founded on the belief that a physical bookstore
remains a vital hub for community, discovery, and the love of reading. Our journey
began many years ago with a passion for literature and a dream of creating a
welcoming space where readers could connect with stories and each other.
We've grown from a small collection of beloved books into a diverse inventory,
always striving to curate titles that inspire, inform, and entertain.
Our commitment is to foster a vibrant literary culture and
provide a haven for book lovers of all ages.

Meet the Owner

Oyegoke Conelius Oyetunji has been an avid reader and advocate for
independent bookstores for over two decades. With a background in
literature and a deep understanding of the publishing world,
he envisioned Independent Bookstore as more than just a place
to buy books; it's a place to explore, learn, and share the joy of reading.
Oyegoke Cornelius Oyetunji is dedicated to fostering a strong relationship
with the local community and ensuring that Independent Bookstore
remains a cherished part of its cultural landscape.
