Common Scenarios / FAQs

"How can I quickly find out what new books have just arrived at the bookstore?"

You can find our latest additions in the "New Arrivals" section on this homepage. We update it regularly!

"I'm looking for a specific genre, and also want staff recommendations. Where can I find these?"

Visit our "Collection" page for categorized genres. For staff picks, check out the dedicated section on the Collection page, updated monthly!

"What are your store hours and how can I sign up for updates?"

All our store hours, address, and a map are available on the "Contact & Location" page. There, you'll also find a signup form for our newsletter.
